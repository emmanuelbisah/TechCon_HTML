Our History

TechCon began over a decade ago as a small meetup for developers and innovators. Since then, it has grown into an international conference that highlights emerging technologies and groundbreaking research.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to bring together technology enthusiasts, professionals, and students to share knowledge, inspire innovation, and shape the future of technology.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the most influential figures in the tech industry:

  • Dr. Ada Lovelace — Pioneer in computing, shared insights on early algorithm design.
  • Alan Turing — Celebrated mathematician, spoke about the foundations of artificial intelligence.
  • Grace Hopper — Computer programming visionary, discussed programming languages and innovation.
